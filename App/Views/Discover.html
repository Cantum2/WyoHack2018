What is Ether Radio?

Etherradio is an open-source and free music streaming service. We are aiming to innovate Soundcloud and Youtube. Our streaming service is home to fan-inspired music, rap battles, and underground performances. We incentivize producers, rap artists, and fans to collaborate to create new music using cryptocurrency.
